Our story

Built from the ground up
after working alongside
specialists across India.

After scaling a healthcare technology company and leading clinical network development for a cardiac AI programme across India, one observation kept recurring:

Clinically exceptional specialists — best outcomes, right equipment, genuine commitment to patient care — watching their procedure volume plateau. Not because their clinical work changed. Because the referring doctors who trusted them had slowly, quietly, drifted toward someone more present.

The gap was not clinical. It was structural. No system existed to manage, measure, and sustain those relationships. ClinCollab is built to be that system.
